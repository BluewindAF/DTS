Terminology

Super Smash Bros. Melee (SSBM) contains a lot of terminology that may confuse newcoming players, so the following are some common terms used while playing and watching the game. This list is not complete, but a very complete list can be found here

1. Tech skill: refers to how precise a player is with high intensity inputs.
2. Air dodge: refers to when a player presses L or R while in the air, redirecting momentum.
3. Wavedash: a movement option done by jumping and then airdodging into the ground.
4. Aerial: an aerial attack. Abbreviated to a direction plus air. Ex. "fair" "dair".
5. L-Cancel: refers to inputting L, R, or Z before hitting the ground with an aerial.
6. Shine: refers to Fox and Falco's down b input, which comes out on frame 1.
7. Waveshine: combination of shine and wavedash which makes Fox/Falco very mobile.
8. Doubleshine: inputting shine, jump, and shine to quickly shine two times.
9. Shinegrab: inputting shine, jump, grab to quickly shine and then grab.
10. Multishine: inputting shine, jump, shine repeadtedly.
11. Jump Cancel (JC): refers to using the jump button to cancel certain inputs.
12. Knockback: how far a hit sends you away.

13. Crouch Cancel: holding down at low percent to reduce knockback.
14. Moonwalk: when the character moves back while facing forward.
15. Waveland: airdodging as you approach the ground to slide.
16. Ledge Dash: a combination of letting go of the ledge and wavelanding.
17. Haxdash: ledge dashing back to the ledge and double jumping.
18. Ledge cancel: using an aerial and then sliding off a platform to make it lagless.
